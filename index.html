Digital Abuse can affect anyone.



Does your partner check your phone?

Do you feel like you are being watched?

Do you feel threatened?

Are you being harassed?

Technology is used by billions of people around the world, a few of which are using it bully, harass and intimidate people.

We want to raise awareness and provide support networks for those experiencing digital abuse.

We also want to help educate those who want to learn more about the common forms of digital abuse and how many people are affected.

We are here to support you with a list of help organisations and charities that are doing tremendous work in combatting digital abuse.
